About Me

Hi! I'm Allison Taylor an Art Director and Graphic Designer from Washington, DC. After discovering a love of graphic design at the age of 16, I traveled all the way to the southern corner of Georgia to get a BA in Graphic Design at Savannah College of Art and Design. With experience in the advertising world and a passion for the arts, I have an exceptional eye for detail and the ability to think critically. Extensive skills in identity, print collateral, production design and interactive design. I enjoy working with clients looking to expand their brand with a strong and individual voice.

I offer the following services:

  • Branding
  • Advertising
  • Photography
